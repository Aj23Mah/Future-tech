About Us
According to the report of Global Hunger Index 2021, Nepal ranks 76th out of the 116
countries with sufficient data to calculate 2021 GHI scores. With a score of 19.1, Nepal
has a level of hunger that is moderate (which is very close to become serious).

In such situation our organization works to feed the needy and undernourished people
of nepal by collecting surplus food from Resturant's, Parties ans other ceremonies and
distribute to the needy people.
