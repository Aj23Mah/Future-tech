About Us

Feed the needy is the non profit organization working to defeat hunger issue in Nepal through surplus food found through various sources and also creating organic fertilizers through utilization of food wastages. Our main aim is to reduce food wastage and utilizing the proper food through distribution and decomposing the decomposable foods, ultimately creating a balanced healthy life for both rich & poor

Objective

  • Eliminating hunger via food distribution program
  • Utlizing the waste food and making proper utilization
  • Reducing pollution via proper management of waste food
  • Providing organic fertilizers to farmers

How we work?

We have created a website that lets connect donors(those who wanna give their surplus food that might be reusable or decomposable)to our organization. We will make the pickup from the location and will distribute the consumable foods to nearby needy people. Unconsumable goods will be disposed in proper way , decomposable goods will be used to make fertilizer
